Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We accept DOCX (Microsoft Word) and Markdown (.md) files. Make sure your chapter headings use Heading 1 (H1) so we can build your table of contents automatically. Apple Pages users: open your file in Pages, then File → Export To → Word, and upload the resulting .docx.

Will my book pass KDP's quality review?

Yes. Every EPUB is automatically validated against EPUB 3 spec and KDP's structural requirements before you download. If anything is wrong, you'll see exactly what before you upload to Amazon.

How long does formatting take?

Most books are formatted in under 5 minutes. You'll get an instant download link as soon as processing completes — and your credit isn't used until you click download, so you can preview the print layout first.

What trim sizes do you support?

We support all common KDP paperback sizes: 5×8", 5.25×8", 5.5×8.5", 6×9" (the standard trade paperback). 8.5×11" is also available for textbooks and workbooks. Select your preferred size during upload — we calculate the correct binding gutter based on your page count.

Do you include front/back matter?

We generate a title page, copyright page, and table of contents automatically. The copyright page uses your name when you fill in the Author field; you can also enter custom copyright text. Front and back matter you include in your manuscript is preserved.

What if I need changes after formatting?

Make your edits in your original DOCX and run it through again. Your credit is only consumed when you actually click Download — the preview is free, so you can iterate on margins, page count, and trim size without burning credits.
